Refund & Policies

Refund Policy

Cancellation Policy
  • Cancellations will only be considered if the request is made within 5 days of placing the order
  • Cancellation requests may not be entertained if orders have been communicated to sellers/merchants
  • Shipyard Nagar does not accept cancellation requests for perishable items
Damaged or Defective Items
  • Report to customer service team within 5 days of receipt
  • Quality complaints must be brought to notice within 5 days
  • Refund processing time: 1 day after approval

Return Policy

Return Eligibility
  • Refund/exchange within first 1 day from purchase date
  • Item must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging must be intact
  • Sale items may not be eligible for return/exchange
Return Process
Process Flow:
1. Return request submitted
2. Product received and inspected
3. Email notification sent
4. Quality check approval
5. Refund/Exchange processed

Shipping Policy

Delivery Information
  • Orders shipped through registered domestic courier companies and/or speed post
  • Orders shipped within 2 days from order date
  • Delivery to address provided at time of purchase
  • Shipping costs are not refundable
Important Notes
  • Platform Owner shall not be liable for any delay in delivery by courier company
  • Delivery confirmation sent to registered email ID
  • Subject to courier company/post office norms
